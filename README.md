## 📊 Project Overview

This Shopping Behavior Analysis Dashboard provides insights into customer purchasing patterns based on data from **3,900 customers**, generating **$233,081 in total revenue**. The dashboard helps understand customer demographics, discount impacts, product preferences, and subscription behavior.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
- **SQL** (MySQL) - Data exploration and analysis
- **Tableau** - Interactive dashboard creation
- **Excel/CSV** - Data cleaning

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing patterns across different age groups and genders
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on purchase behavior
- Identify top-selling products and categories
- Understand size and color preferences by customer segment
- Analyze subscription performance and revenue contribution
- Provide actionable insights for inventory management and marketing

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Inventory Management

1. **Stock High-Performers:**
   - Maintain higher inventory of Blouse, Shirt, Dress, and Jewelry
   - Ensure size M and L always available
   - Stock core colors (Black, Gray) year-round

2. **Seasonal Planning:**
   - Increase Outerwear inventory for winter months
   - Promote Teal and Green for spring/summer
   - Stock holiday-specific accessories (Q4)

3. **Size Optimization:**
   - Expand M and L size ranges (highest demand)
   - Maintain core S and XL for demographic coverage
   - Consider petite and plus-size expansion opportunities

### Marketing Strategies

**By Age Group:**

| Age Group | Strategy | Channel |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Young Adult | Discount-focused campaigns, social media | Instagram, TikTok |
| Middle Adult | Value propositions, email marketing | Email, Facebook |
| Senior | Quality messaging, traditional media | Print, Email |
| Elderly | Convenience-focused, simple navigation | Direct mail |

**By Gender:**
- **Female:** Highlight Clothing and Accessories, feature Teal/Violet colors
- **Male:** Emphasize Clothing and Footwear, showcase Black/Gray options

### Subscription Program Enhancement

1. **Monthly Subscription:**
   - Current best performer
   - Add exclusive benefits to retain subscribers
   - Consider tiered monthly options

2. **Quarterly Subscription:**
   - Target occasional shoppers
   - Include seasonal curation
   - Offer discount on annual commitment

3. **Weekly Subscription:**
   - Re-evaluate pricing and value proposition
   - Target high-frequency shoppers
   - Consider converting to "VIP" program

### Discount Strategy

| Age Group | Discount Approach | Optimal Discount % |
|-----------|------------------|-------------------|
| Young Adult | Aggressive promotions | 20-30% |
| Middle Adult | Targeted offers | 15-20% |
| Senior | Loyalty discounts | 10-15% |
| Elderly | Bundle deals | 5-10% |
